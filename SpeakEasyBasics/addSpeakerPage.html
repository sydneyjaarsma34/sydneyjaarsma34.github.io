How It Works

Enter your speaker's known frequency response

Enter how your speaker changes sound at each frequency band.

Example: If your speaker boosts 8 kHz by +2 dB, move the slider to +2.

SpeakEasy will automatically generate the opposite correction EQ below.

Example:
Speaker response: +2 dB at 8 kHz
Correction EQ: -2 dB at 8 kHz

This makes audio sound more consistent across different speakers.
