Our Mission

Music the way that
you want it

Every speaker system has a unique frequency response. Some speakers have built in bass boosters, and some sound overly bright. Everything is dependent on the circuitry of your specific device.

SpeakEasy builds a unique frequency profile for your specific speaker hardware, then applies real-time correction so that the audio sounds the same across different devices.

Whether you're mixing on studio monitors, listening on a portable Bluetooth speaker, or using your wired headphones, SpeakEasy keeps every playback device equal.
