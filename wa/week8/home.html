Our Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi

The Alpha Iota Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi was established at the University of Colorado Boulder in 1931 and has been in operation ever since. We strive to serve the University of Colorado Boulder's Symphonic Band, Wind Symphony, Concert Band, Campus Band, and Golden Buffalo Marching Band in all capacities.

Our Mission

Kappa Kappa Psi is a diverse fraternity dedicated to advancing college and university bands for the benefit of its members and society through:

Meaningful musical experiences and enrichment;

Transformational leadership development;

Purposeful service to bands and support to band members;

Lifelong fellowship and community.
