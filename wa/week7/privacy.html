Kappa Kappa Privacy Statement

Collected Information

This site does not collect, share, or transmit any personal data to external servers or third parties. The only data stored is your theme preference (light mode or dark mode), which is saved using your browser's local storage. Local storage is a setting saved only on your own device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

How We Use This Data

Your theme preference is stored locally to remember your chosen display mode (light or dark) between visits. This improves your browsing experience by maintaining a consistent visual appearance.

Your Controls

You are in full control of this data. You can:

Switch themes at any time using the “Toggle Theme” button.

Clear local storage in your browser settings to remove all saved data.

Use a private/incognito window to prevent data from being stored entirely.
