Community Garden Project

Join neighbors in creating a shared growing space!

Meeting every Saturday at 10am. Tools provided. All skill levels welcome.

Contact: garden@community.org

Food Pantry

Weekly distribution of fresh groceries and household essentials.

Every Wednesday and Saturday, families can receive fresh produce, canned goods, and basic supplies at the community center.

Contact: pantry@community.org

After School Program

Safe, supervised activities and homework help for students after school hours.

Available Monday through Friday from 3-6pm for elementary and middle school students. Snacks and tutoring provided.

Contact: afterschool@community.org

Senior Services

Transportation, meal delivery, and social activities.

Includes weekly meal deliveries, community outings, and transportation for doctor visits.

Contact: seniors@community.org

Job Training

Skills workshops and career counseling for those currently looking for jobs.

Workshops include resume building, interview prep, and computer literacy classes.

Contact: jobs@community.org

Health Clinic

Basic medical services and wellness programs.

Volunteer nurses offer free checkups and wellness sessions twice weekly at the community center.

Contact: clinic@community.org
